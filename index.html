About Us

Dandy's Parrot Place is an affiliate of the National Parrot Place Organization and is located in Magna, Utah. We raise tropical parrots, from macaws to parakeets. We are just one of more than 300 locations nationwide which are all commited to, "Raising the best companion parrots in a home for a home".

We are as commited to our customers as we are to the babies we raise. We will always be here for you and for the life of your new parrot. We truly love these wonderful, feathered creatures and believe it shows with each one we raise. We strive to ensure and guarantee you the best possible companion parrot in the industry. Please feel free to call or message us for an appointment and/or availability.
